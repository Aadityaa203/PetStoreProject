Happy Pets Store

Here at our Happy Pet Store, we go above and beyond to cater to the needs of both dogs and cats, ensuring they receive top-notch care and attention. For dogs, our services include a comprehensive range of training options, from professional dog training classes to one-on-one obedience sessions. We believe that a well-trained dog is a happy dog, and our experienced trainers are dedicated to helping your canine companion develop good behavior and social skills. Additionally, our vet care services offer everything your furry friend requires to stay in optimal health. From routine wellness exams and vaccinations to specialized treatments and post-operative care, our skilled veterinarians are committed to providing the best medical attention to keep your dog healthy and thriving.

Training
  • Professional dog training classes
  • Personalized obedience training sessions
  • Puppy socialization programs
  • Behavior modification consultations
Vet Care
  • Comprehensive wellness exams

Pet boarding and Daycare
  • Safe and comfortable environment for pets

  • Spacious play areas and cozy resting spots
  • Suitable for both cats and dogs during short or extended periods of absence.

Cats are equally valued members of our pet-loving family, and we have designed a host of services to meet their unique needs. Our grooming services extend to our feline friends, offering full-service grooming that includes bathing, haircuts, and nail trimming. We understand that cats may require a gentler approach, and our groomers are well-trained to ensure your cat's grooming experience is as stress-free and enjoyable as possible. Additionally, our dental care services for cats are designed to maintain their oral health and prevent dental issues. Regular dental exams, cleanings, and recommendations for dental health products ensure your feline's teeth remain in tip-top shape. Whether it's a purring cat or a wagging dog, our pet store provides the finest services to keep your beloved companions happy, healthy, and cherished.
